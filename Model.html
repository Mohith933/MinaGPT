Models

Our Mina models are developed in a structured progression, each adding advanced capabilities over the previous version. Below is a breakdown of each model:

Mina M1

Mina M1 serves as the foundational model in a new lineup of AI designed for simple yet effective interactions. Built to handle basic conversations and tasks, it leverages supervised learning to generate coherent and contextually relevant text responses. One of its key features is its ability to support image and video uploads up to 720p, making it useful for visual-based discussions or content sharing. While Mina M1 isn’t built for highly complex problem-solving or deep analytical tasks, it excels in delivering straightforward answers, assisting with general inquiries, and maintaining casual dialogues. This makes it an ideal choice for users who are looking for a no-frills AI companion that prioritizes ease of use and accessibility. Additionally, its framework ensures efficiency by focusing on fundamental AI principles without overcomplicating its functionality. Whether users want to chat, ask questions, or share media, Mina M1 stands ready to deliver a responsive and adaptive experience, making it a solid stepping stone for more advanced models in the future.

Mina M2

Mina M2 takes the foundation set by M1 and enhances it with advanced capabilities, particularly in image generation. By integrating models like DALL·E, it allows users to create high-quality visuals directly within conversations, expanding creative possibilities. This version also demonstrates improved contextual awareness, making interactions smoother and more intuitive. Mina M2's ability to grasp subtleties in language enables it to engage in smarter and more insightful discussions, bridging the gap between simple responses and more meaningful exchanges. Whether users are looking to brainstorm artistic ideas, generate images, or hold more dynamic conversations, Mina M2 offers an upgraded experience tailored for those who seek both visual creativity and intelligent dialogue. By refining its understanding and response mechanisms, Mina M2 serves as a more sophisticated AI model, opening doors to richer interactions and a deeper connection between users and technology.

Mina M3

Mina M3 represents the pinnacle of this AI series, seamlessly integrating text, image, and video generation into a single cohesive experience. Building on the capabilities of M1 and M2, it introduces video creation using external tools like Sora AI, unlocking new possibilities for storytelling, design, and multimedia projects. Whether crafting compelling narratives, developing artistic concepts, or producing engaging content, Mina M3 is designed to support a wide range of creative endeavors. Its enhanced contextual understanding allows for deeper, more meaningful interactions, making conversations feel more natural and intuitive. By fusing text, images, and videos into a powerful creative suite, Mina M3 stands as a versatile tool tailored for both professionals and casual users who seek innovation and expression in their digital experiences.

Overview

Each iteration of the Mina AI models builds toward a more immersive and multimodal AI experience, seamlessly integrating text, images, and videos to enhance creativity and communication. From the foundational capabilities of Mina M1 to the advanced multimedia generation of Mina M3, every upgrade is designed to push the boundaries of interaction and content creation. The development process is deeply influenced by user feedback and real-world applications, ensuring that each version becomes smarter, more intuitive, and more capable of meeting diverse needs. As AI evolves, so does the Mina series—continuously refined to provide richer experiences and greater possibilities for expression, innovation, and storytelling.
